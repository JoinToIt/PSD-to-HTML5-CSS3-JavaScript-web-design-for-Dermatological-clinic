What is Jaw Slimming or Contouring with muscle relaxants?

With age the skin on our face loses elasticity, being that our face is always exposed to the elements in our environment it is particularly sensitive to this process. Being as it is the thinnest skin on our face this is especially true with the eyelids, it is the first area that begins showing signs of deterioration.

Jaw Line Shaping with muscle relaxants is a refined procedure, aimed to soften facial contours of the jawline and reduce the prominence of an overly strong jaw. It is an essential aspect to bringing facial symmetry and femininity back to the facial structure. While enhancing the aesthetic appeal of the jaw line, the treatment also helps your teeth by preventing teeth grinding from occuring.

How long will the results of the Jaw Line Reshaping last?


The changes after the treatment will be effective with in the first 10-21 days and usually remain for 7-11 months. Some clients may need a second top up to see the complete effect of the treatment. The upside of the treatment is that it is not permanent and corrections to the treatment are always possible.

How much time does the procedure take


The procedure is walk in walk out type procedure, and with a cosmetic doctor it will take approximately 15 minutes. It is the perfect procedure to fit in your lunch break whilst being at work.

What is the safety profile for this procedure

The procedure should always be carried out by a cosmetic doctor or a trained medical doctor. This treatment has been commonly used by cosmetic surgeons and physicians for the last 20 years and no long term side effects have been reported. Moreover the muscle relaxing agent is used to treat children with cerebral palsy and people with over sweating or hyperhydrosis.

Any side effects of the Jaw line Reshaping?

This is a very much a side effect free procedure when done with a trained professional. On rare occasions if the muscle relaxant is injected too high then it is possible for it to effect your smile, for up to 3 months. Depending on person to person the injection site may give redness at the injection site and mild bruising which resides in 24 hours.

Where else can the muscle relaxing agents be used apart from the jaw line?

The injections can be used to treat other common conditions in the body. The most effective treatments are for over sweating or hyperhydrosis, chronic migraines and adding contours and definition to your calf. The effectiveness of the treatments can vary between 3 months to 12 months so it is best to get into contact with a cosmetic physician regarding what treatment suits you best.
