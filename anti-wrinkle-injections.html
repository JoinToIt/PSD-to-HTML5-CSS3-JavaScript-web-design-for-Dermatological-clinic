Anti Wrinkle Injections Melbourne

SKIN CLUB is Melbourne’s premiere clinic for anti-wrinkle treatments. Our Cosmetic Doctors provide subtle and long lasting enhancements to the face. We provide a natural look that aims to wipe of the years and give you a refreshed look.

What are anti wrinkle injections?

Its a natural protein compound that is made in a similar way to penicillin. Bacteria are able to construct this protein and it is injected into the face/body with capability of relaxing the muscles. Anti wrinkle injections are safe at the hands of a Cosmetic Doctor. They are a quick and relatively painless way of stopping the signs of ageing. They come with the backing of university lead research highlighting the fast and natural way it treats ageing skin. Botox ® Melbourne, Dysport®, Xeomin® or other Facial Muscle Relaxants Melbourne.

How do anti wrinkle injections prevent ageing?

Anti Wrinkle injections are known for anti-ageing. They are able to relax the muscle and improve the wrinkles in the skin. By blocking the receptors in the muscle, the muscle becomes more and more relaxed whilst losing its contraction ability. This provides for a better outcome on the surface with skin looking smooth and wrinkle free.

How long do anti wrinkle injection last?
Depending on the injector and on the part of the body, treatments tend to last approximately 4-6 months with some areas lasting up to 12 months. It is important for the patient to be aware of this as anti wrinkle treatment is not permanent.
When will I see results?
With expert injectors and new products you are generally able to start seeing results in 3 weeks time with full results being realized by 6 weeks. However SKIN CLUB has now introduced a new anti wrinkle injection with results being shown in 7 days time. Ask our expert cosmetic doctors for more information.

The face and anti wrinkle injections

Anti wrinkle injections are ideal for the face. They are most effective when the injector has an in depth knowledge of facial movements and anatomy. The following are just a small list where SKIN CLUB’s cosmetic doctors specialise in treatments.

  • Forehead lines
  • Around the eyes – Crows feet
  • Eye brow lifts
  • Jaw reshaping
Being expert injectors our cosmetic doctors are able to inject anti wrinkle injections in innovative ways giving you exceptional results. Using a combination of dermal fillers and anti wrinkle injections, the results can be fantastic. The following is of places of how our cosmetic doctors are setting a new benchmark in anti wrinkle treatment:
  • Sad face improvment
  • Décolletage lines
  • Bugs bunny lines

What does the anti wrinkle injection at Skin Club involve?

The amount of anti wrinkle product injected into the body is very small to cause your body any harm. With expert injectors injecting at SKIN CLUB you will have very little down time and will be able to go on with your day to day duties or work with in the hour. No preparation of the skin is required it is essentially a 15 minute process where our cosmetic doctors will go through an aesthetic assessment and advice you of the treatments that you will require. With in seconds you will have the right amount product injected into your skin and muscle relaxation will start working immediately.

Who should not have anti wrinkle injections?

At SKIN CLUB we regard client safety as our highest priority. Unfortunately our clients who are currently pregnant, breast feeding or have a neuromuscular disorder are unable to have anti wrinkle injections. If you have any further questions then please feel free to discuss this with your cosmetic doctor.
