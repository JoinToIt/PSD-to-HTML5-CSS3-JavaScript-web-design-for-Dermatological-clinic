Dermal Fillers 101

Dermal Fillers are naturally occuring substances called Hyaluronic Acid which are not dangerous but rather in short supply in the skin we age. In the hands of an expert injector we are able to place dermal fillers in the right parts of the face/body hence replacing the loss of Hyaluronic Acid.

What parts do we treat?

Common areas for treatment with dermal fillers include; lips, cheeks, teart, troughs, chin, nose, brows, nasolabial folds, mouth corners, and wrinkles.
