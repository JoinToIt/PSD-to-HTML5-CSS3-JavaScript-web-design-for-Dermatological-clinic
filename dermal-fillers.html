Dermal Fillers 101

Dermal Fillers are naturally occurring substances called Hyaluronic Acid which are not dangerous but rather in short supply in the skin as we age. In the hands of an expert injector we are able to place dermal fillers in the right parts of the face/body hence replacing the loss of Hyaluronic Acid.
SKIN CLUB, dermal filler specialists in Melbourne, Australia has the most reliable products at hand for its customers. We use fillers that have the best reputation and proven results. All our products are able to promote collagen growth and keep volume to the face.

What parts do we treat?

Common areas for treatment with dermal fillers include; lips, cheeks, tear troughs, chin, nose, brows, nasolabial folds, mouth corners, and wrinkles.

How long does it take?

We use Swedish, French and USA made products to provide the best results. Our Doctors pursue the natural look and provide the right amount of treatment so that the results last a minimum of 6-12 months.
