What is Acne?


Acne or “Pimples” is a skin condition caused by irregularities in the sweat glands. As a result of these irregularities there are blockages in your pores resulting in bacterial growth which leads to the formation of pimples. It commonly occurs in teenage years and can persists into the mid 30s if left untreated along with scarring.

What causes Acne?


Changes in the sex hormone levels can cause the body to break out into acne because the sweat glands become more and more active. With this increased activity it is quiet common for the pores to become blocked and allowing a breading ground for bacteria. The bacteria commonly responsible for acne formation is call Propionibacterium acnes or P. acnes.

Goals of Acne Treatment?

With acne forming on the skin of any individual the approach is the same. However with our trained cosmetic doctors who specialise in skin treatments, we are able to reach the root cause of acne and keeping your skin cosmetically perfect.

  • Injectable treatments – Dermal fillers and PRP skin needling are highly specialised techniques to repair scarring
  • Topical treatments – help slow the growth of bacteria and reduce the damage to the skin
  • Oral treatments – certain medications can help with unblocking ppres aswell as attacking bacteria responsible for acne
